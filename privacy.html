BeatRun Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

Overview

BeatRun: Running Music is designed as a simple running music app. The app does not require account creation and does not collect personal data.

Data Collection

BeatRun does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app does not use advertising tracking, analytics SDKs, or third-party login services.

Local App Preferences

BeatRun may store simple preferences on your device, such as language or appearance settings. These preferences stay on your device and are not sent to the developer.
